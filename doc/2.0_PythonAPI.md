## 内部レジスタ操作コマンド
\hrulefill<!-- === === === === === === === === === === === === === === ===  -->

```python
serial2i2c::reg_read(  str registers = "012"
                       )
```
\dotfill  <!-- *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** ***  -->


- **argument**
    - **`registers`**
- **return**
    - str raw_read()

\dotfill  <!-- *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** ***  -->


`registers`のデータをまとめて読み出す

\hrulefill<!-- === === === === === === === === === === === === === === ===  -->

\hrulefill<!-- === === === === === === === === === === === === === === ===  -->

```python
serial2i2c::reg_write( list pair = [ ('1', 0xFF),]
                       )
```
\dotfill  <!-- *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** ***  -->

- **argument**
    - **`pair`** list of touple
        - **`register`** register number
        - **`  data  `** data to write to register
- **return**
    - str raw_read()

\dotfill  <!-- *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** *** ***  -->


それぞれの`register`に、対応する`data`を書き込む

`register`は'0'~'6'もしくは以下の定義済み変数が使える：

    '0'     =>   CHIP_ID
    '1'     =>   GPIO0_STAT
    '2'     =>   GPIO1_STAT
    '4'     =>   GPIO1_CONF
    '3'     =>   GPIO0_CONF
    '5'     =>   I2C_CONF
    '6'     =>   SPI_CONF
